BrainFlash - Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2026

1. Information We Collect

BrainFlash collects minimal information: TikTok account info via OAuth2 and access tokens for publishing content on your behalf.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information solely to authenticate your TikTok account and publish video content as requested by you.

3. Data Storage

All authentication tokens are stored locally on your device. We do not transmit or store your data on external servers.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties.

5. Data Retention

Authentication tokens are retained on your local device until you revoke access or delete the configuration files.

6. Your Rights

You may revoke access at any time through TikTok's settings or by deleting local configuration files.

7. Contact

For privacy questions: brainflashapp@gmail.com
